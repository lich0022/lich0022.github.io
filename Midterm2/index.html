Lichacz Bakery

Business Brief

Locally owned, Lichacz Bakery has been serving residents of Ottawa with delicious baked goods since 2020. We are passionate for baking and strive to make something for everyone. That may be a tough goal to reach, but it's a goal which we will continuously attempt to achieve.

My team and I have brought our passion for baking to the public, so that we can share our love for it with all of you! -Nick Lichacz
