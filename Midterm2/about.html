About

Lichacz Bakery was founded in 2020 during the first COVID-19 lockdown in Ottawa, Ontario.

Meet The Team

Nick Lichacz

Nick founded Lichacz Bakery in 2020. Nick discovered his passion for baking while under the first COVID-19 lockdown in March 2020. He decided to turn is love for baking into a business in his home city of Ottawa.
