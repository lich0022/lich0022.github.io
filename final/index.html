My Love for Photography

I started getting interested in photography when I was in grade 10 or 11. I had mentioned being interested in it, so my parents bought my a camera for my birhday. My first picture with my camera was a picture of my dog, and that is when I truly realized just how much better the imagery was compared to my phone's camera.

Operating Grids

I truly believe that photography is an amazing artform. It is a great way to express one's creativity and individuality. Photography really captures the true beauty of the world, not matter what kinda of photo is being taken.
